Accessible Reads

Let us introduce ourselves...

At Accessible Reads, we believe that everyone should have access to the joy of reading, regardless of their abilities. Our mission is to promote accessibility by offering a wide selection of books in various formats, including large print, braille, audio, and e-books. We strive to support people with disabilities by making reading fun and inclusive for everyone.

We are committed to providing a welcoming and accessible environment for all of our customers. Our knowledgeable staff is always ready to assist with any special needs or requests, and we work closely with publishers to ensure that our selection of books reflects the diverse needs and interests of our community.

At Accessible Reads, we believe that reading is not only a source of entertainment and education, but also a way to promote understanding and empathy. By promoting accessibility and making reading fun for everyone, we hope to create a more inclusive and compassionate society.
